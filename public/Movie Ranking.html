Best Movies and Webseries Summer 2024

Movies and Webseries I Watched this Summer

Panchayat S3

This series takes us to the village of UP and tells us the story of newly positioned Sacheev for the village panchayat.
The story is lighthearted,comedic and portrays the life of the village administration and the struggles of newly appointed sacheev.

8 Show

The series shows the gruesome game consisting of 8 desperate contestants who are willing to do anything to win the game for prize money.

3 Body Problem

The series takes us to abnormal things happening to scientists, as they are suddenly encounterd by a countdown.
